Thit Kho Recipe


Vietnamese caramelized pork, also known as Thit Kho.

Ingredients

  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 2-3 tbsp. brown sugar
  • Canola oil
  • 2 lbs pork shoulder
  • 90 ml fish sauce
  • fresh chili peppers

Steps

  1. Add sugar and canola oil to pan at 9
  2. Once you start seeing a sizzle, change temperature to 6
  3. Once it starts to dissolve and bubble change to 2
  4. When homogenous, add garlic and mix till garlic is fully covered in the mixture
  5. Add pork and mix
  6. Pork juices should be releasing, adjust heat as needed. Juices should still be bubbling.
  7. Once pork is brown, reduce until there's some liquid on the bottom of pan
  8. Add fish sauce
  9. Add 1/2 cup of coconut water if desired
  10. Reduce heat to 5, simmer for at least 30 minutes
  11. Occasionally check and stir
